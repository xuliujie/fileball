Get Started:

Principle of operation:

Players deposit skins in a round.
When the number of required deposited skins is reached, a winner will be picked based on the number of tickets his deposit worth and will be awarded all the skins in the pot!
For every $0.01 a player will get 1 ticket, so a higher number of tickets means a better chance of winning! You can see the details under the Provably Fair section.

How to play?

  1. You should login with your Steam account.
  2. Set your trade link by editing your Steam Profile URL.

    This is a necessary step to receive the items you win and also to deposit skins.

  3. After it you can start to deposit. Verify the total value of your bet and confirm to enter the current round.

    (So you can not accidentally deposit your skins, you have to confirm it on this page!)

  4. Once the item threshold of 50 items is reached, the winner will be announced.
    If you won, you will receive all the items in the pot and you will receive a tradeoffer very soon.
What you can deposit
  • Only Dota2 items from your Steam Dota2 inventory.
  • You can place a total of 12 items in a deposit.
  • Your deposit value must be at least $1.
